INTERNSHIP

Zobuza is always in search of bright students who can actively participate in their projects and warmly welcomes such talent with an internship opportunity.

An internship is a job training for white collar and professional careers. Internships for professional careers are similar in some ways but not as rigorous as apprenticeships for professions, trade and vocational jobs, but the lack of standardisation and oversight leaves the term open to broad interpretation. Interns may be college or university students, high school students, or post-graduate adults. These positions are unpaid
