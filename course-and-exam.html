Course and exam rescheduling policy

In case zobuza reschedules the training event, the options available to the students are

  • The students would have the privilege of rescheduling and attending a class in future at his/her convenience, at any location, on any date of a scheduled training.
  • If the rescheduling request is received 7 days prior (or more), 10% of registration fee will be charged.
  • Please note that rescheduling will be subject to availability of seats.
  • If the request of rescheduling is received within 7 days or less, no rescheduling will be allowed.
  • No refunds.
